# 🌃 Harrata City — A city that never sleeps. Your story might.

> **French-speaking FiveM Roleplay server · Narrative · Whitelist · Free beta**

Welcome to **Harrata City**, a FiveM RP server where **Los Santos becomes a living city again**: every street tells a story of debt, alliance or betrayal. Here, you're not a scoreboard — **you're a resident**.

## 🎭 Our philosophy

We prioritize **narrative, respect for the setting, and slow but meaningful progression**.
Grinding isn't the protagonist: **your character is**.

- **RP quality** first — coherent scenes, fair-play, responsive staff
- **Circular economy** — salaries, inventory, taxes, gray market, real risks
- **Playable factions and companies** — territories, documents, narrative events
- **Seasons & story arcs** — the city evolves with you
- **Anti-cheat + structured logs** for a healthy experience

> ⚠️ **Note**: our server is primarily **French-speaking**, but English-speaking players are welcome. A French-speaking character is recommended but not required — in-character translators exist!
